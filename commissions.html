PORTRAIT COMMISSIONS

I accept a limited number of portrait commissions throughout the year. Each work is created with careful attention to observation, tone, and character, using either oil paint, charcoal or graphite depending on the nature of the image and intended outcome.

These works are developed with the same technical and material standards as my studio practice.

To inquire about a portrait commission please send a request through the Commission Form, or via email with:

  • -A short description of your request
  • -Reference photographs
  • -Preferred size and medium


What I Offer

Portraits based on photographic reference

  • -Single subject
  • -Head-and-shoulders or simplified compositions
  • -Created in either oil paint on canvas or graphite/charcoal on paper

Portrait Commissions are limited each month to ensure consistency with my studio practice.

Each portrait is made with a strong emphasis on likeness, tonal structure, and atmospheric sensitivity.


Mediums

Oil Painting

  • Oil portraits are painted on gallery-wrapped fine linen canvas using only archival artist-grade materials.
  • These works are designed as lasting pieces intended for display and collection.

Graphite or Charcoal Drawing

  • Graphite or Charcoal portraits offer a more immediate tonal interpretation, focusing on gesture, structure and light with a direct visual language.


Sizes

Available standard formats:

  • -30 × 40 cm
  • -40 × 50 cm
  • -Custom sizes may be considered depending on availability.


Pricing

Pricing depends on size and medium. A general guide:

Graphite or Charcoal Portraits

  • -40 × 30 cm: from 9,000 CZK
  • -50 × 40 cm: from 11,000 CZK

Oil portraits

  • -40 × 30 cm: from 18,000 CZK
  • -50 × 40 cm: from 24,000 CZK

A 50% deposit is required to secure a commission slot. The remaining balance is due prior to delivery.

Process

  1. 1. Inquiry -- Submit reference photos and basic details via email or form
  2. 2. Discussion -- We confirm composition, medium, size, and timeline.
  3. 3. Deposit -- A 50% deposit secures your place in the schedule. A compositional sketch will be approved before the work will begin on the canvas or archival paper.
  4. 4. Creation. I work from photographic reference, with updates if needed during key stages.
  5. 5. Completion. Final work is varnished (oil portraits) and carefully packaged for delivery.


Timeline

Portraits typically require several weeks depending on medium, drying time, and current schedule. Exact timing will be agreed upon before starting.
