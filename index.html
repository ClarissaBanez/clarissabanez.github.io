Artist Bio

Clarissa Bañez is a self-taught Filipino artist based in Prague, Czech Republic. She is a woman of many passions. In her childhood, she was formally trained in singing and performed in various music production in Manila, Philippines. She earned her Doctor of Medicine at University of the Philippines in 2013. She pursued various creative interest such as make-up artistry, sewing, graphic design, video production and web development.

In 2022, she relocated to Prague, Czech Republic where she began her taking foundational art courses online and through Výtvarný Atelier Malování Kreslení.

Guided by safe modern painting practices and materials, she is inspired by classical imagery of Caravaggio, Rembrandt, Joh SInger Sargent and Juan Luna. She also has aphantasia, a condition where one is unable to form mental images. Her limitation could not stifle her love for learning. She continues to learn using appropriate classical approaches in developing her skills and techniques.

Currently, she is focusing on exploring still life and portrait painting in oil and digitally while building her first body of work.

Artist Statement

'As an artist, I am committed to weaving narratives through my brushstrokes. My work exemplifies the duality of beauty and emotion, inviting viewers to connect with the stories of women and children depicted in my art. I wish to convey the complex and important role the mother and child bond in society thoughout the times. I strive to resurrect the charm of bygone eras and evoke a sense of wonder about our collective past.'
