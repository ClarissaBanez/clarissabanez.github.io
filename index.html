What I Do

I finished my Medicine Degree in the Philippines, while pursuing various art forms like music and performance, makeup artistry, and design. Since moving to Prague in 2023, I started studying art through New Masters Academy. I am deeply enamored with representational drawing and painting with expressive brush strokes in oil. I am currently making my first body of work and documenting my process to inspire beginning artist like me hone their skill and creativity.

Who I Am

My multitude of hobbies include web development, gardening, baking macarons and bread, and moving my furniture around.
