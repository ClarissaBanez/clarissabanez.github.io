ABOUT

Clarissa Bañez (b. 1989, Manila) is an emerging artist based in Prague, working in . Blending classical techniques with modern, minimally toxic materials, her work explores belonging, resilience, and cultural identity.

An immigrant and mother, she reflects on home as something constantly shaped by relationships, heritage, and change. Her work offers a quiet refuge—an invitation to contemplate identity, memory, and transformation.
